African Gold - Documentary Score by Polle van Genechten

African Gold, Docu

For the last two years, documentary maker Wietske Overdijk has been following DJ and producer Philou Louzolo, capturing every step of the Dutch artist's journey. With roots in Congo, Nigeria and Sierra Leone, Philou has been shaping a forward-thinking blend of house, tech, melodic and afro house since 2016. His 2017 breakthrough led to performances at Berghain, Tomorrowland, Fabric, Awakenings, Lowlands, DGTL, Dekmantel and more, earning support from Black Coffee, Dixon, Solomun, Honey Dijon, Jamie Jones and Laurent Garnier. This rise in popularity also brought a renewed perspective on the music Philou performs, the stages where he feels at home and the vision he wants to share.

Director: Wietske Overdijk
Composer: Polle van Genechten
