Pasará, Fiction

26-year-old Joshua is at a point in his life where he is at a standstill. While his straight friends are making progress in work and relationships, Joshua seems to remain the eternal single. Despite his friends fully accepting him as a gay man, he still lacks a certain recognition. The thought of leaving his hometown to find happiness somewhere else, among like-minded people, haunts his mind more and more. With that thought in mind, he decides to go on a solo trip to Sitges, the gay capital of Europe. Will he finally find the recognition he misses so much among his friends?

In Pasará we show queer youth how you can feel lonely or isolated in a community, but it also teaches how we can help each other feel a little more welcome.

Director: Jarne Hagelaars
Composer: Polle van Genechten
