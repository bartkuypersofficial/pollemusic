Tussen de Regels, Docu

"Tussen de regels" is a short documentary from 2025, part of the series 'Ongeschreven regels' (VPRO/NPO Doc), in which young teenagers explore the grey area between prejudice and the reality of imprisonment. Under the direction of Laura Bokhoven, they examine themes such as punishment, compassion, and the impact of being incarcerated.

Director: Laura Bokhoven
Composer: Polle van Genechten
